Tribute to Abdul Kalam

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, and went on to make significant contributions to the development of India's missile and nuclear programs.

Famous Quotes

  • "Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
  • "We should not give up and we should not allow the problem to defeat us."
  • "Great dreams of great dreamers are always transcended."

Dr. Abdul Kalam will always be remembered for his immense contributions to India's scientific and political landscape.
